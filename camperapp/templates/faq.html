Frequently Asked Questions

Camper+ Basics

What is Camper+?

Camper+ is the prefect toolbox for camp administrators to set up their camp. Camper+ aims to organize all the requirements an administrator would have to do; from registration to creating nametags. Most camps would have to use multiple external sources to gather their information, but with Camper+, everything would be consolidated into one site.

General Users

How can I register as a Administrator?

Administrators will have to email us at Register@camperplus.herukuapp.com to obtain a unique username and password to access their server.

How do Parents sign up?

All parents can click the sign up button on the home page and they will be directed to the registration page. Parents will need a special code that can be found in the Administrators home page.
